The tiger (Panthera tigris) is the largest cat species,reaching a total body length of up to 3.3 m (11 ft)and weighing up to 306 kg (670 lb). Its most recognizable feature is a pattern of dark vertical stripes on reddish-orange fur with a lighter underside. It has exceptionally stout teeth, and the canines are the longest.
